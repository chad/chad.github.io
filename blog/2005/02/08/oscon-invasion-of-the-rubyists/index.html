OSCON - Invasion of the Rubyists

2005.02.08

O’Reilly’s 7th annual Open Source
Convention will be happening on Aug. 1-5 in beautiful Portland, Oregon.

This year, we’re staging a hostile
takeover. If you want to join in on the action, go
signup for a talk (or two).

If you don’t do Ruby, you’re still allowed to submit a
proposal. But, everyone interesting will be in the Ruby track this year.

Update: I forgot to mention that you only have until
Sunday to get your proposals submitted! Sunday.
Don’t procrastinate.
