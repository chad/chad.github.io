MJWTI: Andy Puts It on Wax

2005.08.31

Andy Hunt of the Pragmatic Programmers has
started podcasting.
Much to my delight, he chose to record an excerpt from the introduction to
My Job Went to
India (MJWTI). It’s the very beginning of the book which tells
the story of my first experiences in India (My job did go to
India. I went with it).

Listening to my own words read by someone else (and really well, I might
add) is bizarre. I think the book might actually be better this
way. I could also smell the idlis and sambar (maybe I’m just hungry).

Give it
a listen (or better, subscribe to
Andy’s feed) and let us know what you think.
