Chad Fowler: Comprehensive Profile for Website

Quick Bio

Chad Fowler is a unique polymath who has built remarkable careers in both technology and music. Originally a professional jazz saxophonist from Washington DC, he transitioned to software development without a formal CS degree, becoming a Ruby community leader, bestselling author, venture capitalist, and continuing his avant-garde jazz career through his record label Mahakala Music.
