RubyGems Article in the Rubyist

2005.05.10

Our kind friends at The
Rubyist released their latest edition today. Though I don’t read
Japanese, I’m always sure to find pointers to interesting Rubyisms
via The Rubyist.

This time, they’ve featured the first
in a multipart article series I’m writing on RubyGems. Big thanks to babie for translating!

It’s really cool to see your own words in a language you
(embarrassingly, when you have Japanese heritage, I have to admit)
can’t even read.

For those English speakers who aren’t as easily amused as I am,
I’ve also posted the article in
English over on RubyGarden.
