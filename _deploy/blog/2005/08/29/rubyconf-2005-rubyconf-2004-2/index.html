RubyConf[:2005] > (RubyConf[:2004] * 2)

2005.08.29

Registration for the Fifth International
Ruby Conference is up to greater than two times the
number of the largest conference to-date. And that’s not counting
the usual last-minute surge in registration before the conference.

Check out the
agenda, register, and
(if you or your company have the means) consider sponsoring the conference. O’Reilly is already signed up to sponsor
(thank you!) with a couple more companies lined up.

This is a great chance to meet Matz and many of the leaders of the Ruby
community and the software development industry in general.

Even if you’re not speaking, bring your ideas. Some big ideas (and
code) have come out of conferences past. You’d be surprised what can
be accomplished in between the sessions of a 3-day conference.
